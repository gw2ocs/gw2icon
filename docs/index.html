GW2Icon

Library to insert GW2's icons in your webpages in pure CSS3.

Download

Getting Started #

Installation #

Put css/gw2icon.min.css in your css/ folder and img/gw2icon/ in your img/ folder. Then, in your header, just add: 

<link rel="stylesheet" href="/path/to/css/gw2icon.min.css" />

Usage #

To include an icon in your page, write: 

<i class="gw2-gold-coin"></i>

Sizing #

GW2Icon provides classes to resize the icons:

<i class="gw2-catmander-red"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-3x"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-4x"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-5x"></i>

Transformation #

You can flip the icons:

<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x gw2-flip-horizontal"></i><br/>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x gw2-flip-vertical"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x gw2-flip-horizontal gw2-flip-vertical"></i>

Or even rotate them:

<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x gw2-rotate-90"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x gw2-rotate-180"></i>
<i class="gw2-catmander-red gw2-2x gw2-rotate-270"></i>
